About

Research Engineer at LIG Defence & Aerospace, Seoul — AI for autonomous unmanned systems, in collaboration with Palantir. Oxford MMathCompSci, 2025.

My core research question: can scientific discovery be made computable? Not search — inference. A result buried in a physics paper that silently resolves an open problem in medicine, found by putting both in the same formal language and reasoning across them.

The simulations are the hobby side: agents that grow a language from nothing, a thousand Pokémon species competing in a biome world until the food chains collapse, a statistical reconstruction of the Voynich Manuscript that concludes it probably says nothing at all.

Language Evolution Simulator

ongoing

Agents start with no language and evolve one through selection pressure alone. Currently: ~50-word CVC lexicon, compositional grammar, four emergent grammatical categories, evidentiality, tense — 86% role-decoding accuracy. None of it was designed.

closed
