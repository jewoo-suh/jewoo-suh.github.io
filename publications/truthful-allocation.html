Truthful Task and Resource Allocation in Leader–Follower Agent Organisations

J. Suh et al.
PRIMA 2026  ·  In preparation, 2026

In multi-agent systems where a leader allocates tasks to followers, followers have an incentive to misreport their capabilities. This paper studies the mechanism design problem of making truthful reporting a dominant strategy. We characterise the class of truthful mechanisms for leader–follower allocation, prove impossibility results for certain combinations of desiderata, and propose mechanisms that achieve truthfulness alongside efficiency and fairness properties where possible.
